# eGain Visitor InSite User Guide

Visitor InSite helps a sales user see which accounts are showing interest, understand what they looked at, and decide what to do next.

The app is organized around two main areas:

- **Dashboard:** a list of identified accounts and high-level signals.
- **Account views:** detail pages for one selected account.

## Opening the App

When you open Visitor InSite, you land on the **Dashboard**.

At the top of the page, you will see the eGain logo and the Visitor InSite name. Click the logo at any time to return to the Dashboard.

On desktop, the search box appears in the top bar. On smaller screens, tap the menu/search icon in the top-right area to open search.

## Dashboard

The Dashboard shows recent account activity for the **Last 14 days**.

### Summary Cards

Across the top, four summary cards give you a quick read on activity:

- **Companies Identified:** how many accounts have recent qualifying activity.
- **High Intent Accounts:** accounts with strong signs of buying interest.
- **Repeat Activity:** accounts where activity came back more than once.
- **Accounts Trending Up:** accounts with increasing recent activity.

Hover over the small info icon on a card to see a short explanation.

### Search

Use the search box to narrow the account list.

You can search by company name, website domain, or industry. The account table updates as you type.

### Filters

Above the account list, use the filter buttons to narrow the table:

- **Industry:** show only accounts in one industry.
- **Buying Stage:** show accounts in Awareness, Research, Evaluation, or Purchase.
- **Account Fit:** show High, Medium, or Low fit accounts.

If filters are active, click **Clear all** to return to the full list.

### Identified Accounts Table

The main table lists accounts you may want to review.

Each row shows:

- **Company:** account name and website.
- **Industry:** the company category.
- **Employees:** approximate company size.
- **Revenue:** approximate revenue band.
- **Fit:** how well the account matches the target profile. Higher is better.
- **Intent:** how strongly the account appears to be researching or evaluating. Higher is stronger.
- **Stage:** where the account appears to be in the buying journey.
- **Visitors:** anonymous visitors associated with the account.
- **Action:** a suggested sales cue, such as Follow up, Re-engage, Watch, Nurture, or Check in.
- **Trend:** whether activity is increasing or decreasing.

Click any account row to open that account.

### Signals Panel

The **Signals** panel on the right summarizes notable patterns across accounts. Use it as a quick way to spot themes before choosing where to spend time.

## Account Page

After you click an account, the top of the page changes to that account’s profile.

You will see:

- Company name and buying stage.
- Industry, revenue, employees, location, and website.
- **Account Fit** score.
- **Intent Score**.

Click **Accounts** in the breadcrumb near the top-left to return to the Dashboard.

The account page has four views:

1. **Overview**
2. **Journey Timeline**
3. **Intent Analysis**
4. **Recommended Actions**

Click a view tab to switch between them.

## Overview

The **Overview** tab gives you the fastest account summary.

### AI Executive Summary

At the top, the **AI Executive Summary** explains what matters about the account’s recent behavior.

If a recommended action is available, it appears directly below the summary.

Click **Regenerate** to refresh the AI summary and recommendation. If the button is temporarily unavailable, the app shows when it can be used again.

### Account Activity Metrics

Below the summary, three cards show:

- **Unique visitors:** how many anonymous visitors were seen for the account.
- **Total sessions:** how many visits occurred.
- **Pages per session:** average depth of browsing.

### Top Content Consumed

This section shows the most-viewed content for the account. Use it to understand what topics the account cared about most.

### Identified Visitor Signals

This section lists anonymous visitor labels, their session counts, and how many unique pages they viewed.

These are not personal contact names. They are activity signals connected to the account.

## Journey Timeline

The **Journey Timeline** tab shows the account’s activity grouped into key moments.

Each moment includes:

- Date range.
- A plain-language activity label, such as Research Phase, Pricing Spike, High-intent Visit, or Demo / Trial Evaluation.
- Number of visitors.
- Number of sessions.

Click a moment to expand it.

Inside an expanded moment, click an individual session to see more detail, including:

- Visit date and time.
- Page or activity title.
- Visitor label.
- Session duration.
- Number of pages viewed.
- Additional page details.

Use this tab when you want to understand the story of how the account moved through the site over time.

## Intent Analysis

The **Intent Analysis** tab explains why the account has its current stage and intent level.

### Interest Categories

The left side shows interest categories with scores. Longer bars and higher numbers mean stronger interest in that topic.

### Buying Stage

The right side shows the account’s current stage in the buying journey:

- **Awareness:** early browsing or general discovery.
- **Research:** comparing topics, solutions, or educational content.
- **Evaluation:** looking at stronger buying signals such as product, pricing, demo, or proof points.
- **Purchase:** strongest late-stage intent.

The **Stage rationale** explains why the account is placed in its current stage.

Below that, the small metric cards highlight activity such as pricing/demo sessions, product page sessions, and case studies read.

## Recommended Actions

The **Recommended Actions** tab is where you decide what to do next.

### AI Recommendations

The recommendations are ordered by priority. Each item includes:

- A recommended next step.
- Priority level.
- A short reason for the recommendation.

Click **Regenerate plan** to refresh the recommendations. If regeneration is not currently available, the app shows when it can be used again.

### Suggested Outreach Message

This section contains a drafted message you can use as a starting point.

Click **Copy** to copy the subject and message body. After copying, paste it into your email tool and edit it as needed before sending.

### Suggested Assets to Share

The right side lists helpful eGain assets for the account, such as customer stories, an ebook, an industry overview, or an ROI calculator.

Click an asset to open it in a new browser tab.

## How to Use the App Day to Day

1. Start on the **Dashboard**.
2. Look at **High Intent Accounts**, **Accounts Trending Up**, and the **Action** cues.
3. Use search or filters if you want to focus on a specific industry, stage, or fit level.
4. Open a promising account.
5. Read the **Overview** first.
6. Check the **Journey Timeline** if you need more context.
7. Review **Intent Analysis** to understand the account’s interests and stage.
8. Use **Recommended Actions** to choose your next step, copy an outreach draft, or share a suggested asset.

## Quick Tips

- Higher **Fit** means the account is a better profile match.
- Higher **Intent** means stronger observed interest.
- A positive **Trend** means activity is increasing.
- The **Action** cue is a fast prioritization hint, not a requirement.
- Use AI text as a starting point and adjust it for your own voice before sending.
